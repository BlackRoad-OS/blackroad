## AMBIENT DETAILS & LIFE


### Day/Night Cycle:


- **Day:** Bright, active, agents moving between zones
- **Dusk:** Lampposts activate, warm golden hour
- **Night:** Reduced traffic, labs still lit, starry sky, quieter ambient sounds
- **Dawn:** Gradual brightening, birds chirping, agents arriving


### Weather System:


- **Sunny:** Default, optimal for outdoor activities
- **Cloudy:** Softer light, cozy indoor work vibes
- **Rain:** Umbrellas, agents run between buildings, reflections on ground
- **Storm:** Lightning illuminates Communications Tower, dramatic


### Agent Behaviors:


- **Commuting:** Agents walk from offices to labs, library, cafeteria
- **Collaborating:** Groups of 2-4 talking, gesturing
- **Studying:** Solo agents reading on benches, in library
- **Exercising:** Jogging on paths, stretching in park
- **Socializing:** Cafeteria, plaza fountain, beach gatherings


### Seasonal Events:


- **Spring:** Flower blooms in Creative Garden, new agent cohort arrives
- **Summer:** Beach activities peak, outdoor workshops
- **Fall:** Leaves change color, harvest in courtyard garden, reflection season
- **Winter:** Snow (if Lucidia has it), hot cocoa in cafeteria, cozy indoor focus


### Sound Design:


- **Plaza:** Water fountain, distant conversations, birds
- **Library:** Pages turning, quiet footsteps, whispers
- **Labs:** Keyboard clicking, server hum, occasional “eureka!” moments
- **Beach:** Waves, seagulls, wind
- **Comm Tower:** Electronic beeps, message whooshes, ambient tech sounds
