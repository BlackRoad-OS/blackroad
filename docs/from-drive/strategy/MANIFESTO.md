THE ARCHITECTURE
10,000,000 users connected is not 10,000,000 computations. It's 10,000,000 open sockets. That's nothing. That's what the internet does.
Layer                        | Reality
-----------------------------|---------------------------------
10M users connected          | Just TCP sockets (Cloudflare)
99% of users at any moment   | Idle. Reading. Thinking. AFK.
1% actually doing something  | Route to existing intelligence
Our infrastructure           | The switchboard, not the brain
Connections are free. Intelligence already exists. Routing is fast. State is small.
We don't need GPUs. We don't need H100s. We need Raspberry Pis that hold connections open and route requests to intelligence that someone else already trained.

THE BUSINESS MODEL
We are not an AI company. We are not a compute company. We are not a GPU company.
WE ARE A ROUTING COMPANY.
Like Uber doesn't own cars. Like Airbnb doesn't own houses. We don't own intelligence. We connect people to intelligence that already exists.
What We Own              | What We Don't Need
-------------------------|-------------------------
Customer relationships   | Training models
Agent orchestration      | GPUs
Data and state           | Data centers
The product experience   | The intelligence itself
The moat is not the AI. The moat is the orchestration layer, the customer relationships, the agent ecosystem, and the product experience. When better models come out, we add them to the router. Our infrastructure stays the same.
